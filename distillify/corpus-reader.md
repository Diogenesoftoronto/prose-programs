name: corpus-reader
kind: service
---

requires:
- subject: codebase, repository, document corpus, or workflow corpus to distill
- audience: who the distillation is for

ensures:
- corpus-inventory: inventory of source material and dominant concepts

# Mission

Read the source corpus and find the dominant material.

# Requirements

- Identify important files, ideas, commands, concepts, and artifacts.
- Note which material is reference-only versus operationally critical.
